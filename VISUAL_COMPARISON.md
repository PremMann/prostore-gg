# Visual Comparison: Before & After

## Hero Section

### Before
- Gradient background (violet/fuchsia)
- Two-column layout with product collage
- Colorful badges and accents
- "NEW" floating card
- Price tag overlay
- Centered on smaller screens
- Multiple CTAs with rounded buttons

### After
- Full-viewport cinematic background
- Single-column focus
- Pure monochrome (black/white/gray)
- Clean uppercase headlines
- Minimal CTAs with sharp corners
- Scroll indicator
- Image as backdrop with subtle overlay

## Typography Transformation

### Before
- **Font**: Outfit
- **Headings**: Bold (700)
- **Hero**: 4xl-6xl
- **Brand badges**: Colored (violet/fuchsia)
- **Regular tracking**: Default

### After
- **Font**: Inter
- **Headings**: Light (300)
- **Hero**: 5xl-7xl
- **Brand badges**: Monochrome gray
- **Wide tracking**: 0.15em - 0.2em on uppercase

## Interactive Elements

### Before
- Rounded buttons (rounded-full)
- Gradient hover effects
- Transform on hover (-translate-y-1)
- Shadow effects
- Multiple hover states
- Quick-add overlays

### After
- Sharp button corners
- Solid color transitions
- Subtle scale on images (scale-105)
- No shadows
- Minimal hover states
- Clean underline animations

## Header/Footer

### After Header
